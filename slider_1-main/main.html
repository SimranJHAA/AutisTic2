WHAT IS
AUTISM
Autism, or autism spectrum disorder (ASD), is a complex neurodevelopmental condition that affects communication and behavior, typically appearing by age two. Key characteristics include persistent challenges with social communication and interaction, along with restricted and repetitive patterns of behavior. It is called a "spectrum"
Learning
Tools
Fun, interactive games and activities designed to build communication, motor skills, and emotional understanding in children with autism. Each tool is crafted to support learning at their own pace through visuals, sounds, and play.
Symptoms Of
Autism
Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a neurodevelopmental condition characterized by differences in social communication, behavior, and sensory processing. Symptoms vary widely in type and severity among individuals
